ABOUT TOWNFIT FITNESS & GYM

Townfit was established in 1996 in Central London, Oxford Street. It has soon become a highly rated gym within the city and boasts almost 750 members. It is open 24 hours a day, has great facilities, friendly staff, amazing equipment, and affordable membership.

KEY BENEFITS

OPEN 24 HOURS

TOWNFIT is open 24 hours and is very accessible. Gym instructors work throughout the night and reception area is available if you wish to speak to a member of staff.

CLEAN, SPACIOUS FACILITIES

The facilities have just been upgraded and are immaculately clean and fully spacious.

FREE FITNESS TRAINING

If you become a member, we offer you a free one month fitness training session. This includes free access to our fitness classes and one on one sessions with our fitness instructors.

FRIENDLY STAFF

All of our staff are highly trained and offer great customer service. They are available 24/7 and work around the clock.

EQUIPMENT

Our equipment has just seen an upgrade and we are proud to offer the latest high tech gym equipment.

Flexible membership

We offer great prices for memberships. Please see our membership page for more info.
